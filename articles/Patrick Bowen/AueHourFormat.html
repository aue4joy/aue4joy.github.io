“Aue Hour” meeting format

Patrick Bowen, 2022-02-06

meeting

Here is a meeting format I have devised for a weekly “Aue Hour”.

Inspiration: cghilmo.

  1. start audio recording once there are two or more people in the meeting
  2. verbally gain consent from all members for public recording
  3. wait up to ten minutes for all members who expressed interest to arrive
  4. conduct a reading of Aue
  5. open discussion
  6. after one hour, end the recording
